WHY GUARDIAN

It can't speak.
That's the whole reason we exist.

When people feel unwell, they say so; a pet just endures. An owner's deepest pain isn't the money — it's the uncertainty of "I don't know if it's okay." GUARDIAN does one thing: turn that uncertainty into something you can see, understand, and have caught.

THE NEEDS WE SOLVE

Three real moments,
three needs met.

Moment 01

"Did it slip out again?"

A worker leaves the door open, a leash breaks on a walk, it digs out of the yard at boarding — 90% of escapes happen within 500m of home, and the golden recovery window is just the first hour.

Our answer: geofence alerts the instant it leaves, live location to the meter, route playback for the whole trail. Free for life, built in.

Moment 02

"For my 8 hours at work, what's it doing?"

Drifting off in meetings, checking the home cam on trips, that unease handing it to a sitter — separation anxiety runs both ways, and the anxious one is you.

Our answer: a life-event timeline: when it walked, how long it slept, where it is now. No news is the best news.

Moment 03

"When we find out, will it be too late?"

A pet's instinct to hide pain makes chronic change nearly invisible. A routine exam only shows "the day it got sick" — no one can answer the vet's "what's it usually like."

Our answer: a 365-day continuous baseline that catches the faint signals of chronic change, so you don't miss the moment to ask the vet.*

* Nudges do not constitute a medical diagnosis; consult a licensed vet for diagnosis and treatment.

OUR UNIQUE VALUE

Four things others can't do

① Longitudinal data:
from "dots" to a "line"

A routine exam sees "the day it got sick" — isolated dots; GUARDIAN holds 365 days of a continuous line. One continuous baseline beats ten isolated snapshots — an information edge no single check-up can replace, and our deepest moat.

Guardianat check-up

Dou Dou's 365-day health record

Activity rhythm · sleep quality · HRV trend · temperature curve — the vet sees, for the first time, "what it's usually like," not just "what it's like when sick."

Export PDF for the vet

② An individual baseline, no generic thresholds

A Corgi's "normal" and a Border Collie's "normal" are nothing alike. We model each pet on its own — not against other dogs, but against its own yesterday. That takes time and data; latecomers can't catch up overnight.

③ A philosophy of restraint

We only interrupt you when a trend persistently crosses a statistical threshold (e.g. 3 standard deviations); single blips are logged silently. Real user data: just 2 pushes in 200 days. No crying wolf — that's how you guard for years.

④ Explainable and traceable

Every nudge spells out "why": deviation size, duration, whether it's appeared before. No black-box verdicts, no mystic scores — because trust is only bought with transparency.

⑤ Data sovereignty is yours

Three-tier data management; sensitive physiological data only flows with your explicit authorization. Export anytime, revoke anytime. Your pet's data is always your asset.
