GOVERNANCE

Restraint is our compliance strategy —
and our business strategy.

PRINCIPLE 01

A nudge ≠ a diagnosis

Strictly defined as a health nudge, not a medical diagnosis; diagnosis and disposition belong to licensed vets — sidestepping device-approval and misdiagnosis risk at the product-definition level.

PRINCIPLE 02

AI holds no final authority

Irreversible decisions like medical judgment and insurance adjudication must be made by licensed professionals or humans; the core chain rejects black-box models — fully explainable and auditable.

PRINCIPLE 03

Tiered data governance

L1 basic / L2 private / L3 sensitive management, compliant with GDPR and local law; sensitive data flows only with explicit user authorization, partners access only the service-relevant window.

PRINCIPLE 04

Asset-light compliance path

Insurance starts in white-label/TPA mode early on, touching no capital pool or prescription rights; channels stay neutral and multi-sourced, with the core consumer interface and distribution rights in hand.
